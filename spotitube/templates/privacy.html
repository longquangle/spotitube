Privacy

SpotiTube: We respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your information when you use our website and services.

Information We Collect

Personal Information: We may collect personal information such as your Spotify username, your Spotify playlists, and Spotify/YouTube credentials when you use our services. This information is necessary to facilitate the playlist transfer process.

Usage Data: We DO NOT collect non-personal information about your interaction with our website, such as your IP address, browser type, and device information.

How We Use Your Information

Playlist Transfer: We use your Spotify/YouTube credentials to access and transfer your playlists between the two platforms.

Service Improvement: We analyze usage data to enhance our website's functionality and user experience.
